Joinly Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Joinly ("the Bot", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our Discord bot.

By using Joinly, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

Joinly is a Discord bot that provides server management features, including customizable welcome messages, goodbye messages, configuration tools, backups, and other server utilities.

To provide these features, Joinly may collect and store limited information from Discord.

We only collect information that is necessary to operate, maintain, and improve the Bot.

2. Information We Collect

Discord Server Information

We may collect and store:

Server ID
Server name
Server configuration settings

Why we collect this:

Server IDs are required to identify your server and store its settings correctly.

Server names may be stored for easier identification in our systems, logs, and support processes.

Configuration data is stored so Joinly can provide features such as welcome messages, goodbye messages, and other server settings.

Discord User Information

We may collect and store:

Discord User IDs
Discord usernames

Why we collect this:

User IDs are used for identifying users when required for features such as:

Premium ownership
Permissions
Support requests
Configuration ownership
User-specific settings

Usernames may be stored to make logs and support information easier to understand.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information collected by Joinly may be used to:

Provide and maintain Bot features.
Store server configurations.
Troubleshoot technical issues.
Improve performance and reliability.
Provide customer support.
Manage Premium services.
Prevent abuse or misuse of the Bot.

We do not use collected information for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

We take reasonable steps to protect stored information from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.

However, no online service can guarantee complete security.

Data is stored only for as long as necessary to:

Provide Joinly's services.
Fulfill operational requirements.

5. Data Sharing

Joinly does not sell, rent, or trade your information.

We do not share stored information with third parties except when:

Required to comply with legal obligations.
Necessary to protect the security of Joinly or its users.
Required to provide a requested service.

6. Third-Party Services

Joinly relies on third-party services to operate, including:

Discord's API
Hosting providers
Database providers
Payment providers for Premium services

These services may process limited information required for Joinly to function.

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policies.

7. Data Deletion Requests

You may request deletion of data stored by Joinly.

To request data deletion:

Join our Support Server:

https://discord.gg/PTrU8DQacj

Open a support request.
Provide the necessary information to identify your data.

We may request verification to ensure that the deletion request is legitimate.

Once approved, applicable stored data will be deleted within a reasonable timeframe.

8. Removing Joinly

Removing Joinly from your Discord server will stop future data collection from that server.

However, previously stored information may remain until it is:

Deleted through a data deletion request.
Removed through normal data retention processes.

9. Children's Privacy

Joinly does not knowingly collect personal information from users who are below Discord's minimum required age.

If you believe a minor's information has been collected, please contact us through our Support Server.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of Joinly after changes are made means you accept the updated Privacy Policy.

11. Contact & Support

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our Support Server:

https://discord.gg/PTrU8DQacj

By using Joinly, you acknowledge that you:

Understand how your information is collected.
Understand how your information is used.
Accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
